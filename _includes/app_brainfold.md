Challenge your brain and battle your friends in real-time trivia showdowns. **Brainfold Battle** lets you create custom quizzes, compete head-to-head, and climb the leaderboard.

### Why Brainfold Battle?

Trivia is more fun when it's personal. **Brainfold Battle** lets you build quizzes on any topic, challenge friends in real time, and see who really knows their stuff. Whether it's study prep, team bonding, or just bragging rights — the battle is on.
