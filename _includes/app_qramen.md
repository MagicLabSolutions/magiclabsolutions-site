### Why QRamen?

QR codes are everywhere — menus, tickets, Wi-Fi passwords, event links — but managing them shouldn't be a hassle. **QRamen** makes it effortless to scan, create, customize, and organize all your QR codes in one beautiful app.

### QRamen Pro

Unlock the full power of QRamen with Pro:

* **Unlimited QR Codes** — Create as many QR codes as you need
* **Custom Colors & Styles** — Brand your QR codes with any color and style
* **Logo Overlay** — Add your logo to the center of QR codes
* **QR Analytics** — Track scans, opens, shares and more
* **Folders & Organization** — Keep QR codes organized in folders
* **Bulk CSV Import** — Generate QR codes from CSV files

### Available Everywhere

QRamen works across all your Apple devices:

* **iPhone** — Full-featured scanning, creation, and management
* **iPad** — Optimized layout with split-view support
* **Apple Watch** — Quick access to your saved QR codes on your wrist
* **Home Screen Widgets** — Access your most-used QR codes instantly
