Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Designed by parents, for parents. Zuzu makes baby tracking intuitive, insightful, and genuinely helpful.

Visual Timeline Arc

See your baby's entire day at a glance with an intuitive circular timeline. Instantly spot patterns in sleep, feeds, and activities.

AI-Powered Insights

Get personalized analysis and predictions powered by AI. Understand your baby's routines and receive smart recommendations.

Quick Action Logging

Log feeds, diapers, sleep, and more with a single tap. Nursing timer, bottle tracking, and custom event types built right in.

Growth & Milestones

Track weight, height, and head circumference with WHO growth charts. Record milestones and see how your baby is progressing.

Smart Sleep Predictions

Know when your baby will be tired next. Sleep predictions adapt to your baby's unique rhythm and improve over time.

Widgets & Apple Watch

Check timers and recent events without opening the app. Home screen widgets and Apple Watch complication keep info at your fingertips.
