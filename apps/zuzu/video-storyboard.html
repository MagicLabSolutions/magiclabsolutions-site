⌨ Typography

  • Primary Font: SF Pro Display / system sans-serif
  • Headline Weight: Bold (700) or Heavy (800)
  • Body Weight: Regular (400) or Medium (500)
  • Text Color: White (#FFFFFF) on dark backgrounds
  • Subtext Color: Muted purple-gray (#9898B0)
  • Text Size: Large, always readable at phone scale (min 48px at 1080w)
  • Text Shadow: Subtle drop-shadow for readability over screenshots
  • Alignment: Center-aligned for overlays, left-aligned for captions

🎵 Music & Audio

  • Style: Gentle, warm acoustic lullaby feel
  • Instruments: Soft piano, music box, light acoustic guitar, subtle strings
  • Tempo: Slow to medium (~80–100 BPM), calming
  • Mood: Nurturing, hopeful, tender — captures the joy of early parenthood
  • Structure: Intro swell (0:00–0:03), steady groove (0:03–0:24), gentle resolution (0:24–0:30)
  • Licensing: Royalty-free required — check Artlist, Epidemic Sound, or Musicbed
  • SFX: Optional soft tap sounds on button interactions, gentle whoosh on transitions
  • No voiceover — text overlays carry the message; music only

⚙ Technical Specs

  • Resolution: 1080 × 1920 px (9:16 vertical)
  • Frame Rate: 30 fps
  • Duration: 30 seconds exactly
  • Codec: H.264 (Main or High profile)
  • Container: .mp4 or .mov
  • Bitrate: 10–15 Mbps for high quality
  • Audio: AAC, 48 kHz, stereo
  • Color Space: sRGB (Rec. 709)
  • Platform: Apple App Store product page video
  • Safe Area: Keep key text within center 90% — avoid top/bottom 5% for system UI overlap

⇄ Transition Guide

  • Dissolve: 0.4s cross-fade between scenes, used for mood shifts
  • Cross-dissolve: 0.3s blend, used for same-context screen changes
  • Swipe Left: 0.5s horizontal slide with slight parallax on background, mimics in-app navigation
  • Easing: Use ease-out or spring curves for all motion (never linear)
  • Overlap: 2–3 frames of overlap between outgoing and incoming scenes
  • Text Timing: Text overlays appear 0.3s after scene visual settles, fade out 0.3s before transition

☑ Delivery Checklist

  • Verify all text is readable at 375pt width (iPhone SE minimum)
  • Ensure no text falls outside the safe area margins
  • Confirm total runtime is exactly 30.00 seconds (900 frames at 30fps)
  • Export with no black frames at start or end
  • Validate H.264 encoding passes App Store Connect checks
  • Test playback on iPhone, iPad, and desktop preview
  • Confirm music licensing covers App Store distribution
  • Review Apple's App Preview guidelines for compliance
