Features

Your complete
QR toolkit

Everything you need to scan, create, organize, and track QR codes in one powerful app.

Scan & Save Instantly

Point your camera at any QR code and it's recognized instantly. Import from your photo library or other apps. Every scan is automatically saved to your collection for easy access later.

Create Custom QR Codes

Generate QR codes for Wi-Fi networks, contacts, URLs, calendar events, and more. Customize with your brand colors, add your logo, and choose from beautiful templates.

Organize with Folders & Tags

Keep your QR codes organized exactly the way you want. Create folders, add tags, and use powerful search to find any code in seconds. Never lose a QR code again.

QR Analytics

Track how your QR codes are being used with detailed analytics. See scan counts, open rates, and share history. Understand your audience and optimize your codes.
